AMERICAN DIABETES ASSOCIATION (ADA) STANDARDS OF CARE 2024 - NUTRITION CONSENSUS

1. MEDICAL NUTRITION THERAPY (MNT)
   - No "one-size-fits-all" percentage for carbs/protein/fat. Distribution must be individualized.
   - Weight Loss: >5% weight loss recommended for overweight/obese patients.

2. DIETARY PATTERNS
   - Effective Patterns: Mediterranean, DASH, Vegetarian/Vegan, Low-Fat.
   - Key Elements: Nonstarchy vegetables, whole fruits, legumes, nuts, seeds.
   - Avoid: Sugar-sweetened beverages, refined grains, ultra-processed foods.

3. CARBOHYDRATE MANAGEMENT
   - Focus on carbohydrate *quality* (high fiber, low GI) over just quantity.
   - Fiber Goal: >14g/1000kcal.

4. FATS (CARDIOVASCULAR HEALTH)
   - Prioritize: Monounsaturated (Olive oil, nuts) and Polyunsaturated fats (Fatty fish, seeds).
   - Limit: Saturated fats <10% of energy.
   - Supplements: Omega-3 supplements not routinely recommended (get from food).

5. CLINICAL GOALS (REFERENCE)
   - HbA1c Target: Generally <7.0% for non-pregnant adults (individualized).
   - Blood Pressure: <130/80 mmHg is generally preferred for high CV risk.
   - Lipid Profile: LDL <70 mg/dL (or <55 mg/dL for very high risk).
