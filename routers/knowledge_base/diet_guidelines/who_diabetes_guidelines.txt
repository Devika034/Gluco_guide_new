WORLD HEALTH ORGANIZATION (WHO) DIABETES DIETARY GUIDELINES

1. CORE PRINCIPLES
   - Achieve and maintain healthy body weight.
   - Be physically active (at least 30 mins regular, moderate-intensity activity).

2. DIETARY ADJUSTMENTS
   - Sugars: Free sugars should be <10% of total energy intake (ideally <5%).
   - Fruits/Vegetables: At least 5 servings (400g) per day.
   - Fats: Total fat <30% of total energy. Shift from saturated to unsaturated fats.
   - Trans-fats: Eliminate industrial trans-fats.

3. SPECIFIC RECOMMENDATIONS
   - Complex Carbohydrates: Choose whole grains, legumes, vegetables.
   - Glycemic Index: Low GI foods are preferred to prevent rapid glucose spikes.
   - Sodium: <2000mg per day (<5g salt).

4. PREVENTION
   - Healthy diet combined with physical activity significantly reduces Type 2 Diabetes risk.
